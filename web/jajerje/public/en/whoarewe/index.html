About us

JAJERJE studios is a small group of seven members born out of the need to complete a project for the subject Introduction to Software Engineering. Faced with the question of what project to undertake and the indecision that consumed us, we decided (to try) to create a clicker in Unity. We had never used Unity or C# before, but we accepted the challenge nonetheless.

The result is what you can try on this very website by scrolling to the Download me! section and following the steps. Although humble, I hope it meets your expectations, dear reader.

As a fun fact, JAJERJE gets its name from the initials of its seven members:

  1. Jorge
  2. Artur
  3. Juan Manuel
  4. Eduardo
